Biography

Linus Torvalds is a Finnish- American software engineer who is the creator and principal developer of the Linux kernel for a number of operating system. He also created the version control system Git. Torvalds was born on December 28,1969 in Helsinki,Finland. He studied computer science at the University of Helsinki, and developed the Linux Kernal as a student projectin 1991. Since then,The Linux Kernel has become one of the most widely used Kernels in the world,powering everything from smartphgone to supercomputers.
